    Biography

    I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Michigan State University, supervised by Professor Guan-Hua Tu and Professor Li Xiao. Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in 2018. My research interests are in the broad areas of security, mobile networks and systems, IoT, formal method, and vehicle to everything (V2X), with a recent emphasis on securing and innovating 5G and beyond mobile network protocols and technologies..
